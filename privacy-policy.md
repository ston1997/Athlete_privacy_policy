# Privacy Policy

**Athlete**
Last updated: March 13, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Athlete ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using Athlete, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address and authentication credentials (via Firebase Authentication)
- Profile information you provide (name, age, gender, weight, height)

### Workout & Fitness Data
- Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weight, duration)
- Weekly training plans and schedules
- Personal records and progress data
- Body measurements (weight, waist, chest, hips, bicep)

### Health Data (Apple HealthKit)
- With your explicit permission, we access Apple HealthKit data including: steps, heart rate, active energy, sleep analysis, and workout data
- HealthKit data is used solely to display health insights within the app
- We do NOT store HealthKit data on external servers — it remains on your device and in Apple Health
- We do NOT share HealthKit data with third parties
- We do NOT use HealthKit data for advertising

### Food & Nutrition Data
- Photos of food items you choose to analyze (processed via Google Cloud Vision and OpenAI APIs for nutritional analysis)
- Calorie and nutritional information derived from food analysis

### AI-Generated Content
- When you use AI features (AI Trainer, AI Progress Analysis), your workout data may be sent to OpenAI API to generate personalized recommendations
- This data is processed in real-time and is subject to [OpenAI's privacy policy](https://openai.com/privacy)

### Advertising Data
- We use Google AdMob to display advertisements to free-tier users
- AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA) and usage data to serve relevant ads
- You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings > Privacy > Tracking)

### Payment Information
- Payments are processed through Stripe
- We do NOT store your credit card details — all payment processing is handled securely by Stripe
- We only receive confirmation of your subscription status

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the app's functionality
- To track your workouts, progress, and fitness goals
- To generate AI-powered training recommendations and progress analysis
- To analyze food photos for nutritional information
- To display relevant advertisements (free-tier users)
- To process premium subscription payments

## Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication, Cloud Storage)
- Data is transmitted using encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
- Access to your data is restricted to your authenticated account

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We share data only with:

- **Google Firebase** — for data storage and authentication
- **Google AdMob** — for displaying advertisements (device identifiers only)
- **Google Cloud Vision** — for food image analysis (photos only, when you use calorie tracking)
- **OpenAI** — for AI-powered features (workout data for generating recommendations)
- **Stripe** — for payment processing (payment details only)

All third-party services are bound by their own privacy policies and data protection obligations.

## Data Retention

- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent for data processing
- Opt out of personalized advertising

## Children's Privacy

Athlete is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app.
